About Study Book

Empowering engineering students with the best study resources.

Our Mission

Study Book is a community-driven platform designed exclusively for engineering students. Our primary mission is to provide free, high-quality, and easily accessible notes, previous year questions (PYQs), and comprehensive syllabus guides to help you achieve your academic goals.

Why We Built This

We understand the struggle of scrambling for notes right before exams. By centralizing study materials that are often scattered across various drives, chats, and websites, we aim to save students valuable time. Knowledge grows when shared, and Study Book is built on the foundation of students helping students.

Join the Community

We encourage everyone to not just consume, but also contribute. Have great handwritten notes? Upload them and help thousands of your peers succeed.

About Study Book

Empowering students with organized resources for academic success.

About Us

Study Book is a learning platform built especially for B.Tech students, with a strong focus on Computer Science and related branches.

As engineering students ourselves, we know the real problems — scattered notes, unclear syllabus structure, outdated resources, and the constant pressure of exams, internals, and placements. Study Book was created to solve these problems by providing well-organized, reliable, and easy-to-access study material in one place.

What We Provide

  • Semester-wise and subject-wise B.Tech notes
  • Previous Year Questions (PYQs) to understand exam patterns and important topics
  • Clear syllabus breakdowns for better planning and coverage
  • Helpful study tools to improve focus, consistency, and productivity

All content is structured to match university-level curriculum, making it easier for students to study efficiently and revise faster.

Built for CSE Students

Study Book is especially designed for Computer Science students, covering core subjects such as:

  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Operating Systems
  • Database Management Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Object-Oriented Programming
  • Core programming subjects

Our goal is to help students build strong fundamentals, not just pass exams.

Our Vision

We aim to make Study Book a trusted academic companion for B.Tech students — from understanding core subjects to preparing confidently for exams and beyond.

We are continuously working to improve the platform by adding better resources, smarter tools, and a smoother learning experience, based on real student needs.
