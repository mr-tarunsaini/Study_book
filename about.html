About Study Book

Empowering engineering students with the best study resources.

Our Mission

Study Book is a community-driven platform designed exclusively for engineering students. Our primary mission is to provide free, high-quality, and easily accessible notes, previous year questions (PYQs), and comprehensive syllabus guides to help you achieve your academic goals.

Why We Built This

We understand the struggle of scrambling for notes right before exams. By centralizing study materials that are often scattered across various drives, chats, and websites, we aim to save students valuable time. Knowledge grows when shared, and Study Book is built on the foundation of students helping students.

Join the Community

We encourage everyone to not just consume, but also contribute. Have great handwritten notes? Upload them and help thousands of your peers succeed.
