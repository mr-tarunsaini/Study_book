Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your data.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your basic profile information such as your Name, Email Address, and authentication data. If you choose to update your profile, we may also collect your College, Course, and Branch details to personalize your experience. We do not collect sensitive payment or location data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is primarily used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, it allows us to track the PDFs you have saved, manage the notes you upload to the community, and suggest study materials that match your engineering branch.

3. Data Storage & Security

We use Google Firebase to securely store user data and uploaded documents. Industry-standard security measures are implemented to ensure your account and files remain secure. However, please remember that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Cookies

Study Book utilizes basic local storage and essential cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your UI preferences (such as Dark/Light mode and your "Continue Reading" history).

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us via the Contact Us page.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Study Book.
Your privacy is important to us. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it. We only collect data that is necessary and provided by you voluntarily.

2. Information We Collect

Important Information

We collect the following information only when you submit a form:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message or query

This information is used only to respond to you.

Optional Information

You may choose to share:

  • Course / branch
  • College or university name
  • Notes or PYQs contribution links

Providing optional information is completely your choice and not required to use the website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only to:

  • Reply to your messages
  • Improve website content
  • Review and manage contributed notes or PYQs

We do not sell, share, or misuse your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Forms to collect responses.
Google may handle data according to its own privacy policy. Study Book only accesses the responses submitted to us.

Our website may also contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the Contact Us page.
