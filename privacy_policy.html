Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your data.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your basic profile information such as your Name, Email Address, and authentication data. If you choose to update your profile, we may also collect your College, Course, and Branch details to personalize your experience. We do not collect sensitive payment or location data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is primarily used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, it allows us to track the PDFs you have saved, manage the notes you upload to the community, and suggest study materials that match your engineering branch.

3. Data Storage & Security

We use Google Firebase to securely store user data and uploaded documents. Industry-standard security measures are implemented to ensure your account and files remain secure. However, please remember that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Cookies

Study Book utilizes basic local storage and essential cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your UI preferences (such as Dark/Light mode and your "Continue Reading" history).

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us via the Contact Us page.
