Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Study Book.
Your privacy is important to us. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it. We only collect data that is necessary and provided by you voluntarily.

2. Information We Collect

Important Information

We collect the following information only when you submit a form:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message or query

This information is used only to respond to you.

Optional Information

You may choose to share:

  • Course / branch
  • College or university name
  • Notes or PYQs contribution links

Providing optional information is completely your choice and not required to use the website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only to:

  • Reply to your messages
  • Improve website content
  • Review and manage contributed notes or PYQs

We do not sell, share, or misuse your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Forms to collect responses.
Google may handle data according to its own privacy policy. Study Book only accesses the responses submitted to us.

Our website may also contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the Contact Us page.
